About us

The Trivandrum Bar Association , the District representative of the apex bodies namelyBar Council of Kerala and Bar Council of India, has its primary motto is to serve equally its members, the profession and the public by defending liberty and help delivering justice. The Trivandrum Bar Association achieves its mission through tireless work toward five goals such as Serve its Members, Improve Our Profession and to Promote the Rule of Law. The Trivandrum Bar Associationprovide benefits to its members, conducts welfare and educational programs, seminars and give relentless service which ensures its membersethical conduct, professional growth and quality of life.Itpreserves the independence of the legal profession and the judiciary.
